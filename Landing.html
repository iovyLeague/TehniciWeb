The Chefs

Silvia Barruco, a Michelin star chef, developed her culinary skills in her teens under her grandmother's guidance in southern Italy. After studying at the Culinary Arts Academy in Rome, she refined her talents working with Chef Christophe Pelé at Le Clarence in Paris and later with Chef Alain Ducasse at Le Meurice Alain Ducasse. Following six transformative years with Chef Ducasse, Silvia fulfilled her dream of opening her own fine-dining restaurant, The Experience - At Silvia's, in the heart of Tuscany. Here, she crafts a culinary journey that marries Italian tradition with modern innovation, celebrating the essence of regional cuisine with every dish.

The Process Behind THE EXPERIENCE - At Silvia's

The Experience at Silvia's is not just a restaurant; it's a journey that transcends borders and cultures, curated with a touch of Italian magic. Conceived with the vision of offering a culinary experience that transports guests through multiple countries, Chef Silvia has crafted a menu that surprises and delights, while preserving her rich cultural heritage. Nestled in the heart of Tuscany, where Silvia once sought solace in the tranquil dolce far niente lifestyle during summer breaks, the restaurant exudes an ambiance of relaxed elegance. Here, amidst the rolling hills and vineyards, Silvia channels the same carefree spirit into her dishes, aiming to recreate that sense of blissful indulgence with every bite. At The Experience at Silvia's, guests embark on a gastronomic journey that celebrates the beauty of simplicity and the art of savoring life's pleasures.

Frequent Questions

  • Do you host private events?

    Yes, we do host private events for up to 100 people. For inquiries and to discuss your event needs, please contact us. We would be delighted to assist you in creating a memorable experience.

  • Do you serve à la carte?

    Yes, we do. Our à la carte menu offers a selection of exquisite dishes crafted with the finest ingredients and culinary expertise.

  • Where do you source you wines?

    We take great pride in sourcing our wines from renowned vineyards, with a focus on Italian wines from the picturesque region of Tuscany. Each bottle is carefully selected to complement our dishes and enhance the dining experience.

  • Can you accommodate dietary restrictions or allergies?

    Absolutely. We strive to accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies to ensure that every guest enjoys a memorable dining experience. Please inform us of any restrictions or allergies when making your reservation, and our team will be happy to tailor our menu to suit your needs.

  • What is your cancellation policy for reservations?

    We kindly request that you notify us of any reservation cancellations at least a week in advance. However, we understand that emergencies can arise unexpectedly. Please contact us as soon as possible in such cases, and we will do our best to accommodate your needs.

  • Do you have a dress code?

    We highly recommend that guests embrace an elegant style when dining with us. Our restaurant exudes an atmosphere of sophistication, and we believe that dressing appropriately adds to the overall dining experience.
